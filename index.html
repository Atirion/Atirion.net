About me

I'm a part-time streamer who delights in entertaining others.

I'm based in Norway and have been playing games since your grandparents were born. It's been a long time but one of my earliest memories is playing Tomb Raider on the Playstation console.

Without a doubt, my favorite game from all of my gaming experience is Dark Souls. For some reason I loved the thrill of being knocked into a cycle of hopelessness.

Visit my channel and watch me play a wide variety of different games; including World of Warcraft, Counter-Strike, Amnesia and many others that are either suggested by my viewers or spontaneously spring to my mind.

Hopefully this small introduction into me, myself and my games has given you some insight into what to expect when you visit.

Come and watch LIVE on Twitch!
